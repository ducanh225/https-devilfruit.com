Home > Paramecia > Toshi Toshi no Mi

Statistics

Japanese Name: トシトシの実

Official English Name: Age-Age Fruit

Meaning: Age; Year

Usage Debut: Chapter 499; Episode 393

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Jewelry Bonney

Description

The Toshi Toshi no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to manipulate the ages of people or objects, making them an Age Manipulation Human (年齢自在人間, Nenrei Jizai Ningen). It was eaten by Jewelry Bonney.

Etymology

Toshi (年, 歳) is Japanese for "age" or "year".

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

The main strength of the fruit is that it allows the user to freely manipulate the aging process of anything, including themselves, other people, animals, and objects. The years the user steals from people are manifested in the form of sparkling jewelry and shapes.

The user can disguise themselves or cripple opponents by rapidly aging their targets into their senior years or regressing them into small, weak children. This ability requires only an instance of physical contact for its effects to apply.

Weaknesses

The aging effects are temporary, meaning victims will eventually revert to their rightful age. The user must maintain their adult form, and overexertion can cause them to revert back to their real age. Additionally, there are targets immune to the fruit's ability, as Bonney could not use her ability on Jaygarcia Saturn.

While the user can transform into any possible future form, the more aware they are of reality, the narrower the future possibilities become. The user suffers from the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses, and it is implied that submerging the user might cause the effects to be nullified.

Usage

Bonney mainly uses her ability to maintain the appearance of a woman in her early 20s in public. She can fool people into believing she is someone else by shifting her apparent age up or down. Bonney has displayed innovative uses of her fruit's capabilities, such as puncturing walls to create doorways or using her powers in combat to change the age of her enemies.

Techniques

Distortion Future: Bonney ages herself up into a possible future version, gaining a large and muscular physique. This technique can also be applied to others.

Distortion Future: Nika Mitaina Mirai: Bonney ages herself up into a possible future version, gaining a physique like Nika, allowing her to stretch and enlarge her arm.

Distortion Future: Kyojin Mitaina Mirai: Bonney ages herself into a giant-like form, although she cannot maintain it for long.

Toshi Tsuki: Bonney thrusts a weapon into a target, aging them into a younger version of themselves, with the lost years manifesting as jewels.

NDE (Near-Death Experience): Bonney fires a projectile that causes the target to rapidly age into a skeleton before reverting to their initial state.

History

While Bonney was a baby in Mary Geoise, Jaygarcia Saturn performed an experiment on her to test whether a Devil Fruit could be given to an infant via an extract. Although Bonney acquired the power, the fruit itself was rendered useless. Following the Solo Revolution in the Sorbet Kingdom at age 6, Bonney subconsciously accessed the fruit's power, leading to confusion about its capabilities. It was only during the Egghead Incident that Saturn revealed to Bonney how he granted her the power.

Trivia

This fruit has the longest gap between when its power was first shown and when it was named, at 15 years, 5 months, and 2 weeks. The Toshi Toshi no Mi is similar to Kuma's Nikyu Nikyu no Mi and Big Mom's Soru Soru no Mi, as it can extract and sublimate intangible aspects of a living target. The aging and decaying power of this fruit is akin to the Juku Juku no Mi, with the main difference being that the latter's effects are irreversible.

Bonney's ability to mimic the powers of another Devil Fruit is notable, particularly when she used Distortion Future: Nika Mitaina Mirai to access abilities similar to the Gomu Gomu no Mi. Interestingly, her first usage of a Gear 3-like Nika power parallels Luffy's first use of Gear 3 in combat against a Cipher Pol agent.
