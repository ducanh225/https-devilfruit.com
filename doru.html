Home > Paramecia > Doru Doru no Mi

Statistics

Japanese Name: ドルドルの実

Official English Name: Wax-Wax Fruit

Fruit of the Doru-Doru (Odex)

Meaning: Candle

Fruit Debut: One Piece Magazine Vol.9

Usage Debut: Chapter 118; Episode 71

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Galdino (Mr. 3)

Description

The Doru Doru no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create large quantities of candle wax from their body and control it, making the user a Candle Human (ろうそくキャンドル人間, Kyandoru Ningen, VIZ: "Human Candle", "Wax Man"). It was eaten by Galdino, better known by his Baroque Works alias, Mr. 3.

Etymology

"Doru" comes from the Japanese word for "candle" (キャンドル, kyandoru). In the Viz Manga, and the 4Kids and Funimation dubs, it is called the Wax-Wax Fruit. In the Odex dub, it's called the Fruit of the Doru-Doru.

Appearance

The Doru Doru no Mi is a white, pear-shaped fruit with the top part having a melted appearance, similar to a candle. It has a green stem at the top that has one droplet-shaped extrusion on each end, making it look like wax dripping, and the typical Devil Fruit swirl patterns spread throughout.

Strengths and Weaknesses

The major strength of the fruit, as demonstrated by Mr. 3, is that the user is able to produce candle wax from their body and mold it into any shape they wish. When the wax hardens, it becomes as strong as steel. The wax is also resilient to certain acidity, such as the stomach acid of Bananawani and Magellan's Hydra. The user's hair becomes ignited when the fruit's power is activated, making the user resemble a candle. The fire does not harm the user, though.

A piece of hardened wax can be broken by being struck with another piece of hardened wax, causing both pieces of wax to crumble. Despite its density, the wax will still melt if exposed to flames or heat, including the fire burning on Mr. 3's head. Also, with the user being a "candle-man" due to the fruit's powers, placing the user in an environment with a significant amount of heat can greatly weaken them as a result. Other than that, the user is also affected by the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses.

Usage

The fruit's bestowed powers, as used by Mr. 3, have been used mostly in techniques that comply with Mr. 3's artistic sense. These range from wax statues that completely resemble other people in every little detail to a gigantic wax cake complete with burning candles. Amongst these techniques, Mr. 3 most notably uses the fruit's powers to encase his enemies with wax in order to turn them into living wax statues.

Mr. 3 can also create keys with his wax, which proved to be an invaluable asset in breaking out of prison cells and shackles, even if they are made of Seastone. How Mr. 3 knows the key's exact structure and can reproduce it completely is yet unknown. Another use for his powers is to power his ship, similar to certain Logia-class users, though his exact method is unknown.

Techniques

Candle Lock

Candle Lock (キャンドルロック, Kyandoru Rokku): Producing wax and spreading it over an opponent's limb, Mr. 3 immobilizes his opponents with a large candle-shaped restraint. This was first seen being used as a named technique against Vivi.

Tokudai Candle Service Set

Tokudai Candle Service Set (特大キャンドル･サービスセット, Tokudai Kyandoru Sābisu Setto): Mr. 3 creates a massive birthday cake-shaped wax arrangement with a pillar on top of the lower "floors" of the cake, then makes a spinning half-dome on top of that, with burning candles. He then places targets he has captured onto the cake. As the candles burn, they slowly encase the targets in wax vapor, and essentially turn them into wax statues. If left unchecked for too long, the targets suffocate.

Candle Jacket

Candle Jacket (キャンドルジャケット, Kyandoru Jaketto): Mr. 3 uses wax to tie up his opponents in the ground. This was seen being used on Brogy.

Doru Doru Arts "Ken"

Doru Doru Arts "Ken" (ドルドル彫刻アーツ「剣」, Doru Doru Ātsu 「Ken」): Mr. 3 makes a large sword out of wax. This was first seen being used to pin Brogy's hand to the ground to prevent the giant from escaping.

Doru Doru Arts "Mori"

Doru Doru Arts "Mori" (ドルドル彫刻アーツ「銛」, Doru Doru Ātsu 「Mori」): Mr. 3 makes a wax harpoon and attacks an enemy with it. This was first seen being used against Luffy.

Candle Wall

Candle Wall (キャンドル壁ウォール, Kyandoru Wōru): Mr. 3 makes a wax wall to defend himself from an enemy that is attacking him. This is strong enough to withstand most blunt attacks. This was first seen being used against Luffy.

Candle Champion

Candle Champion (キャンドル･チャンピオン, Kyandoru Chanpion): Covering himself in wax, Mr. 3 makes a massive battle suit that resembles a large robotic boxer. The suit greatly enhances his physical power and defenses.

Doru Doru no Yakata

Doru Doru no Yakata (ドルドルの館, Doru Doru no Yakata): Mr. 3 creates many wax clones of himself in order to conceal his real body and launch a fatal surprise attack on the target with a dagger.

Doru Doru Ball

Doru Doru Ball (ドルドル･ボール, Doru Doru Bōru): Mr. 3 covers himself in wax to protect himself. This was first seen being used to protect himself against the stomach acid of the Bananawani after it ate him.

Candle House

Candle House (キャンドル・ハウス, Kyandoru Hausu): Mr. 3 creates a big box to get in with small holes to be able to see. It was used to move safely on Level 5 alongside Buggy.

Doru Doru Kaijo

Doru Doru Kaijo (ドルドル解除, Doru Doru Kaijō): Mr. 3 turns back into liquid the hardened wax of anything he created. This was first seen being used to remove the Doru Doru Candle Armor he created for Luffy.

Combo Techniques with Luffy

While the powers of the Doru Doru no Mi have been used exclusively by Mr. 3, they have also been used inadvertently by Luffy. Discovered when Mr. 3 encased his feet in wax, Luffy uses the hardened substance in conjunction with his own Devil Fruit powers to deliver attacks strengthened by Mr. 3's wax.

Non-Canon Techniques

Doru Doru Arts "Tejo" (ドルドル彫刻アーツ「 手錠」, Doru Doru Ātsu 「Tejō」): Mr. 3 creates wax restraints that can pin the hands of a giant-sized target to the ground. This was used to restrain Brogy in the anime instead of the swords in the manga.

Candle Shield (キャンドル・シールド, Kyandoru Shīrudo): Similar to Candle Wall, Mr. 3 creates a rather rudimentary crude shield with his wax powers. It was first used in order to protect Buggy from being attacked by the Wolf Unit of Impel Down Level 5.

Candle Coating (キャンドルコーティング, Kyandoru Kōtingu): Similar to Candle Wall, Mr. 3 creates a coating of wax to block harmful substances from reaching him and his allies. It was first used to protect himself, Buggy, and the released prisoners from Level 2 of Impel Down from the poison that Magellan used to block the exit.

Doru Doru Arts "Ono" (ドルドル彫刻アーツ「斧」, Doru Doru Ātsu 「Ono」): Mr. 3 creates a battleaxe out of wax and uses it to attack enemies. This appears in Pirate Warriors 3.

Doru Doru Arts "Taiho" (ドルドル彫刻アーツ「大砲」, Doru Doru Ātsu 「Taihō」): Mr. 3 creates a cannon out of wax and fires a shot at enemies. This appears in Pirate Warriors 3.

Super Candle: Ultimate Champion ("スーパーキャンドル"!! "アルテイメツトチャンピオン", "Sūpākyandoru"!! "Aruteimetsutochanpion"): Galdino creates Candle Champion armor for himself, Marianne, Mikita, and Gem. The four of them then dock together, creating a giant Candle Champion.

Trivia

In a particular scene in the manga, after being beaten by Sanji, Mr. 3 is seen floating on water as if he did not possess any Devil Fruit powers at all. Oda responded to this scene in an SBS, explaining that Mr. 3 was kept afloat by a piece of "Unbelievably Floaty Wood" underneath him. In the anime version of the scene, Mr. 3 was instead held afloat by an overturned table.

This fruit is often one of the few Paramecia confused as a Logia-class Devil Fruit, because it seemingly produces an "element". However, it is not a Logia because the user cannot transform into the said element, but rather just control it. Despite this, in the anime, Mr. 3 seems to literally melt due to the intense heat of Level 3 in Impel Down, though it is probably intended as a comic effect to get across how much Mr. 3 dislikes hotter environments.

This fruit is also one of six shown that have the capability to enable the users to traverse the sea, with the other five being Ace's Mera Mera no Mi (via the Striker), Smoker's Moku Moku no Mi (via the Billower Bike), Perospero's Pero Pero no Mi (via the Candy Seaslug), Charlotte Linlin's Soru Soru no Mi (via Homies that are unaffected by bodies of liquids) and Aokiji's Hie Hie no Mi (via the Ao Chari). How Mr. 3 does this is not fully explained, but he is said to have used his abilities to power his ship.
