<td width=80%> <font size=20 face=“tahoma” color=black> Devil Fruits are supernatural fruits that are scattered throughout the world. Any living being who eats one will gain a unique ability at the cost of becoming weakened in bodies of water, resulting in them losing the ability to swim. With one notable exception, beings are limited to acquiring one Devil Fruit power.<br>

Devil Fruits are classified into three types: <a href=01.html> Paramecia </a> fruits grant a vast array of generally "superhuman" abilities;<a href=02.html> Zoan</a> fruits grant the power to transform into animals; and<a href=03.html> Logia </a> fruits, the rarest and most powerful, grant the power to transform one's body into a natural element. Each fruit is also given a name based on the power it grants and has a unique appearance, which can be used to identify it. One fruit, in particular, has the honor of having two names and being identified with two types.<br>

Devil Fruits are very rare in the world, enough to be considered myths in much of the Four Blues, but they are much more common in the Grand Line and have been consumed by many prominent figures among the Three Great Powers. The abilities they bestow coupled with their rarity cause Devil Fruits to be highly sought-after; single specimens may be valued at up to several billion belly, and extensive efforts have been made to replicate their powers and mass-produce artificial versions for warfare. The scientist Dr. Vegapunk has performed the most extensive research into Devil Fruits and has achieved numerous unnatural applications and replications of their abilities, but not even he knows for certain how they originated</font></td></center>
