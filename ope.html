Statistics

Japanese Name: オペオペの実

Official English Name: Op-Op Fruit

Meaning: Operation

Fruit Debut: Chapter 765; Episode 704

Usage Debut: Chapter 504; Episode 398

Type: Paramecia

Previous User: A certain doctor

Current User: Trafalgar D. Water Law

Description

The Ope Ope no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit whose ability is to create a spherical domain, inside of which the user can freely rearrange, dismantle (without harm), and generally remodel/restructure anything and anyone (themself included) in a "surgical" way, making the user a Free Modification Human (改造自在人間, Kaizō Jizai Ningen). The fruit was eaten by Trafalgar D. Water Law, having been force-fed to him by Donquixote Rosinante. Before Law, the fruit's powers were held by a certain famous doctor.

Etymology

Ope is short for operēshon (オペレーション), the Japanese pronunciation of the English word "operation" (as in, surgical operations). In the VIZ manga and Funimation adaptations, it is called the Op-Op Fruit, "Op" being also short for "Operation".

Appearance

The Ope Ope no Mi is a red, heart-shaped fruit resembling a strawberry, small enough for a man to hold it in-between his fingers. It has a stem with a swirling end pointing upwards, and, as characteristic of natural Devil Fruits, its surface is swirl-patterned.

Strengths

The Ope Ope no Mi allows its user to "remodel" the world around them as if by "operating" on it. By conjuring a transparent-blue, spherical domain of sorts that is centered around the user (dubbed "ROOM" by Law), they gain total control over everything inside that territory, from living beings—including themself—to nonliving objects. Examples of the ways in which the user can "surgically" manipulate things within their ROOM include swapping the locations of two targets to teleport them, levitating and moving objects of any size around akin to telekinesis, "scanning" targets, slicing people/objects apart without actual injury, and then reattaching separated parts to other surfaces.

Awakening

Once this fruit is "awakened", the user gains the ability to create special ROOMs that adhere directly to foreign objects or living beings instead of surrounding a specific space around the user, not requiring their presence within the operating field for techniques to be performed.

Weaknesses

The fruit's most obvious drawback is that the user can only use its powers within a specific spherical area that they have to summon themself, so if either the user or the target move outside the area, they are unaffected by its powers. While a big enough ROOM tends to be hard to notice, smaller ones can be easily detected, allowing an enemy to spot it and keep their distance.

While the fruit provides access to a multitude of abilities, most of them have unique limitations of their own. For example, the user is required to complete hand gestures in order to use certain attacks. A major weakness of this fruit is that usage of the fruit's abilities depletes the user's stamina, resulting in situations where the user must rest after or avoid long or multiple enemy confrontations.

It was also hinted by Smoker that a Logia's intangibility may offer some level of protection from the fruit's powers. However, if a Logia user has a part of them severed by this power, that part loses the effects of the user's Devil Fruit, meaning it can be harmed without the Logia fruit protecting it from damage. Additionally, the user is affected by the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses.

Usage

Previous User

In the past, when a certain doctor ate the fruit, the doctor was able to perform miraculous operations that saved many lives and cured all sorts of diseases in ways that no ordinary surgeon was capable of.

Trafalgar D. Water Law

Law is overall a tricky and smart fighter, using his various abilities to redirect attacks coming at him, disarm opponents, or escape perilous situations. He has developed this ability to a great extent over the timeskip, as his ROOM now covers a much larger area than it did before. Law typically uses his Devil Fruit powers in tandem with his swordsmanship, making his sword a "scalpel" of sorts while his powers are active.

Techniques

ROOM: The precursor to all of Law's techniques, Law creates a spherical space around himself and his targets, in which he can manipulate anything he wants in a variety of ways.

Amputate: Law's basic offensive technique, allowing him to cleanly cut his target without actually harming them.

Radio Knife: An advanced version of Amputate, where Law charges his sword with electricity before slashing his enemy, resulting in the target being separated into several segments while also suffering an electric shock.

Shambles: Within the space created with ROOM, Law can swap the positions of any object inside, which can be used for both offensive and defensive purposes.

Gamma Knife: Law's strongest non-Awakened technique, which destroys his victim's internal organs without leaving any external wounds.

History

According to Diez Barrels, this fruit was once eaten by a doctor in the past who became a legend due to the miraculous operations he performed. After being force-fed to Law by Rosinante, Law used the fruit's powers to save himself from Amber Lead Syndrome and has since utilized its abilities in various battles.

Trivia

Most of Law's techniques reference real medical terms or surgical tools. The Ope Ope no Mi is not the only Devil Fruit with the ability to provide eternal youth, as the Hobi Hobi no Mi grants the same effect to the user upon consumption.
