Home > Paramecia > Shima Shima no Mi

Statistics

Japanese Name: シマシマの実

Official English Name: Isle-Isle Fruit

Meaning: Island

Usage Debut: Chapter 1080; Episode 1113

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Avalo Pizarro

Description

The Shima Shima no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that grants the ability to merge with an island's environment and manipulate it, making the user an Island Human (島人間, Shima Ningen, VIZ: "Island-Man"). It was eaten by Avalo Pizarro.

Etymology

Shima (島) is Japanese for "island".

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

Consuming the fruit grants the user the ability to assimilate themselves with the environment of any island, whether natural or artificial, and then manipulate said environment freely. For example, the user can merge themselves with a mountainside or floors built from wood, as long as they are in contact with them.

Depending on the size of the island, the user can become a fearsome opponent thanks to the sheer scale of their ability's range. Even cannon fire from a Marine battleship is not sufficient to hurt or delay the user when they are merged with a large enough island. The user can further manipulate the landmarks they are assimilated with to perform actions such as emoting or talking, or manifesting limbs that can interact with objects. The user is also sensitive to the actions of people that act upon the environment they are merged with, which can be applied for surveillance purposes.

Weaknesses

The fruit's most noteworthy weakness comes from the fact that since the user is effectively fused with the island they are controlling, any significant damage dealt to the assimilated island's environment will be reflected on the user's true body while they are merged together. Enemies with immense physical strength or great-enough Busoushoku Haki can exploit this by attacking any part of the island that is being manipulated.

As indicated by the fruit's name, it is unknown if the user has any limitations regarding non-island locations. Avalo Pizarro's consumption of the fruit has yet to show any form of control of the landmass beyond the coastline of Hachinosu, likely due to it being underwater. Otherwise, they are susceptible to all the common weaknesses among Devil Fruit users.

Usage

Avalo Pizarro utilizes his fruit's ability to become one with the island of Hachinosu, assimilating the skull-shaped rock formation at the center of the island to serve as his main means of communicating with the island's inhabitants. He can also detect prison breakouts from the dungeons through an itching sensation, allowing him to quickly warn others.

In battle, Pizarro can manifest gigantic arms from Hachinosu's ground and the buildings upon it that are able to reach and crush large ships at sea with ease. He also uses this ability to pick up his own crew's ships and bring them directly to their headquarters for convenience.

History

At the time that Monkey D. Garp and the members of SWORD came to Hachinosu to rescue Koby, Pizarro stated that his Devil Fruit was a new power, suggesting he acquired his ability shortly before then, likely as a result of the Blackbeard Pirates' Devil Fruit hunting.

Trivia

This Devil Fruit's ability is similar to the Ishi Ishi no Mi's ability, which allows the user to merge with and possess their environment; however, unlike the Ishi Ishi no Mi's ability to merge with stone-based constructs, this Devil Fruit allows the user to merge with any environment. That being said, this Devil Fruit's user is susceptible to sensations and damage applied to the environment they are merged with, a weakness not present in the Ishi Ishi no Mi.

This Devil Fruit's weakness, where the damage inflicted on the user's environment is shared with their real body, is similar to that of the Shiro Shiro no Mi, where the damage inflicted to the user's outward fortress body is shared with their inner manifestation of their real body.
