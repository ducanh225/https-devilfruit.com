Statistics

Japanese Name: ボムボムの実

Official English Name: Bomb-Bomb Fruit (4Kids, Funimation, Odex); Boom-Boom Fruit (Viz)

Meaning: Bomb

Fruit Debut: One Piece Magazine Vol.11

Usage Debut: Chapter 110; Episode 65

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Gem (Mr. 5)

Description

The Bomu Bomu no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to make any part of their body explode, whether it be their limbs, hair, mucus, or even breath or cough, making the user a Bomb Human (爆弾人間, Bakudan Ningen, VIZ: "Human Dynamite"). It was eaten by Gem, better known by his Baroque Works alias Mr. 5.

Etymology

"Bomu" is the Japanese spoken word for "bomb." It is called the Boom-Boom Fruit in the Viz manga, reflecting the sound bombs make, and the Bomb-Bomb Fruit in the 4Kids, Funimation, and Odex dub.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

The fruit's main strength is that the user can detonate their entire body or limbs, generating powerful explosive forces that harm anything but the user. The explosive quality extends to bodily emissions such as mucus and breath, allowing for ranged attacks.

The fruit grants immunity against all other explosions, including cannonballs, and allows the user to evaluate the quality of explosives. For example, Gem commented that Usopp's Gunpowder Star tasted bad due to low-quality gunpowder.

Weaknesses

The ability only activates when the user initiates contact with something or at will, as both Zoro and Luffy have been seen hitting Gem without him exploding. Despite the user's explosion immunity, they are not particularly resistant to fire. If the user takes significant wounds, they will lose control of any mucus they generate, leaving them vulnerable to further attacks. Other than that, the user is affected by the standard weaknesses of Devil Fruits.

Usage

Gem uses his powers to pick his own mucus and flick it at opponents for long-range strikes. For close range, he can detonate his arms, legs, or all of his body to inflict damage. Despite the explosions not harming him or his clothes, he prefers to pull up his sleeves before using his arms to blow someone up directly.

Gem often fights in conjunction with Mikita, as his explosions provide enough impulse for her to fly around. He generally does not react against explosive projectiles, knowing they will not harm him, and may even ingest them to contain the explosion and protect his allies. However, he can be tricked into eating something that looks like an explosive but is not.

Gem has access to a Flintlock .44 Caliber 6 Shot Revolver, allowing him to weaponize his explosive breath into functional bullets by blowing air into the gun's chambers and firing normally. He claims to dislike using this against enemies he perceives as weak, but will do so if feeling impatient.

Techniques

The named techniques that involve the Devil Fruit powers include:

  • Nose Fancy Cannon: Gem digs a dried mucus out of his nose and flicks it at the opponent, causing it to explode on contact.
  • Nose Fancy Double Cannon: Similar to the Nose Fancy Cannon, but he flicks mucus with both hands simultaneously.
  • Kick Bomb: Gem kicks his opponent, detonating his leg on impact.
  • Breeze Breath Bomb: Gem blows into the chambers of a revolver pistol, firing his breath as invisible exploding bullets.
  • Zenshin Kibaku: Gem grabs his opponent in a bear-hug and detonates his entire body at once, creating a highly destructive explosion.

    • Trivia

    This ability is similar to the Pamu Pamu no Mi and the non-canon Basu Basu no Mi, as they all involve explosions. The main difference is that the Bomu Bomu no Mi is limited to the user's body, while the others can affect external objects.
