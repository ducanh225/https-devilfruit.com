Paramecia
The most common Devil Fruit type, Paramecia, encompasses fruits that grant any one of a wide variety of "superhuman" abilities. Their one unifying characteristic is that they are anything other than the ability to transform into animals or into elements of nature.
Some Paramecia fruits allow one to transform oneself or adopt a special bodily trait, temporarily or permanently (e.g., gaining a slippery physique, a body that can split into pieces harmlessly, a diamond body, becoming a jacket to be worn by others, transforming into blades and other weapons, controlling one's weight, mimicking appearances, gaining clairvoyant eyes or a brain with infinite storage capacity, etc.).
Other abilities involve creating substances from nothing, some of which may have strange properties (e.g., wax as hard as steel, soap that can "clean off" strength, poison, strings that can control people like puppets, straw with "voodoo" properties, etc.). Other fruits' powers involve creating and manipulating intangible forces (like "quake" shockwaves, gravity, or heat) and yet other abilities involve summoning discrete objects or entities (e.g., copies of one's own body parts, ghosts, a "genie" familiar, articles of clothing, etc.).
Other Paramecia powers allow wielders to influence and alter their environment, both living beings (sometimes including their own self) and/or objects (e.g., turning people into toys, controlling people's shadows or souls, "taming" animals, controlling specific objects like books or mirrors, making things levitate, making projectiles auto-lock onto targets, "surgically" modifying anything within one's domain, etc.).
