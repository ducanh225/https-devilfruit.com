Home > Paramecia > Wara Wara no Mi

Statistics

Japanese Name: ワラワラの実

Official English Name: Straw-Straw Fruit

Meaning: Straw

Usage Debut: Chapter 508; Episode 401

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Basil Hawkins

Description

The Wara Wara no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create and manipulate straw at will, with the straw having damage-redirecting properties. It was eaten by Basil Hawkins.

Etymology

Wara (藁) is Japanese for "straw".

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

The fruit's main ability is the capacity to create and manipulate straw, which can serve as a holding device or act as a sharp weapon. The user can create a large straw monster to attack foes and can manifest nails to use as weapons.

Hawkins can create small straw dolls that represent other people, allowing him to redirect damage he receives to the doll instead. This ability makes him difficult to defeat, as he can survive multiple life-threatening injuries if he has enough dolls in store.

Weaknesses

The user can only carry a maximum of ten dolls in their body, leaving them vulnerable once all dolls are lost. If the person the doll represents suffers damage, the doll is damaged too, leaving it unusable. Additionally, the straw created by the fruit's power is not very durable, as it can be easily sliced through.

Aside from these weaknesses, the user suffers from the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses.

Usage

Hawkins primarily uses his fruit's powers in conjunction with his cartomancy and voodoo magic. He can create straw dolls to protect himself from sudden enemies and can use the lives of people his enemies care about as leverage in battle.

Techniques

Straw Man: Hawkins creates a small straw doll that represents another person and stores it within his body, allowing him to redirect damage to the person the doll represents.

"Life" Minus: When Hawkins suffers an injury, the damage gets redirected to one of the dolls inside his body, and in turn to the person the doll represents.

Goma no So: Hawkins covers his body with straw, giving him the appearance of a giant scarecrow-like monster with metal nails serving as claws.

Straw Man's Card: Hawkins generates a large amount of straw from his sword, creating a large straw monster that he can control remotely.

Trivia

The voodoo-esque straw dolls created with this fruit are possibly based on "wara ningyō" (藁人形), a popular kind of Japanese katashiro ("substitute") doll made of straw. The main effect of Hawkins' Devil Fruit appears to be based on a mix between stereotypical voodoo curses and the ritual known as ushi no toki mairi (丑の時参り), which involves practitioners inflicting imminent death on victims by nailing a wara ningyō representing them onto a tree during a specific night time.

Hawkins' Goma no So technique has slightly different designs between the manga and anime. In the former, this form does not have feet, instead moving around in straw tentacles. In the anime, however, Hawkins retains his human feet, although turned into straw.
