Statistics

Japanese Name: バラバラの実

Official English Name: Chop-Chop Fruit

Other Name: The Fruit of Dismemberment (Odex)

Meaning: Scattered; in pieces

Fruit Debut: Chapter 19; Episode 8

Usage Debut: Chapter 9; Episode 5

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Buggy

Description

The Bara Bara no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to split their own body into pieces and control said pieces however they wish, as well as making the user immune to slashing attacks, making the user a Splitting Human (バラバラ人間, Barabara Ningen). It was eaten by Buggy, albeit accidentally, as he originally intended to sell it. Its power was first demonstrated when Buggy lifted and choked one of his subordinates via his dismembered hand.

Etymology

Barabara (バラバラ/ばらばら) is a Japanese onomatopoeia meaning disconnected, disperse, in pieces, or asunder. Barabaraninaru (バラバラになる) means "to come/fall apart." More specifically, the fruit's workings can also refer to a body in pieces, e.g., barabarajiken (ばらばら事件), meaning "a murder case in which the victim is chopped into pieces." In the VIZ manga, 4Kids, and Funimation dubs, it is called the Chop-Chop Fruit.

Appearance

The Bara Bara no Mi is an oval-shaped orange-red fruit composed of small spherical parts with swirls, and a green leaf sprouting on top. It is small enough to be held by one hand. Its overall appearance resembles an oversized raspberry or a miniature pineapple.

Strengths

The fruit's major strength is that the user becomes permanently immune to damage from being cut or pierced, regardless of what form. Instead of the body parts being permanently dismembered from such attacks, the dismembered parts of the user's body remain under the user's control. They can then be freely manipulated to execute unique attacks or reattach them back to the body's remaining portion. In the anime, Buggy has demonstrated being able to reconstitute himself in a Logia-like manner after being reduced to fine strips by Dracule Mihawk.

Aside from this passive immunity, the user can also manually split their body apart without the need of being chopped up by others. This ability is convenient during battles, allowing the user to attack through enemy blocking attempts or avoid incoming moves as long as they see it coming. The user can live without being connected to vital limbs and organs for long periods, as evidenced by Buggy's Crew Adventure Chronicles.

Additionally, the levitation effect given by the freeform control of the parts seems to extend to things in contact with the user, allowing them to bypass gravity by lifting objects regardless of their weight. Buggy has shown the ability to easily lift individuals much larger than himself, such as the Blue Gorillas and Jinbe.

Weaknesses

Though the user is immune to slashing and piercing attacks, they are still susceptible to other forms of engagement, such as blunt attacks like punches and kicks. This can be mitigated by having the attacked body part "fly off" to deflect the force of the attack. Even when a body part is separated, the user can still feel sensations of whatever happens to that part, which can be a major weakness as an enemy can perform various actions on the separated body parts, including tickling or tying them up.

The user can only control their separated pieces up to a certain distance. If a part goes beyond this distance, the user loses all control of that part, although vital functions from that body part continue. The distance is a 200 Bara Bara diameter sphere around the point where the person touches the ground. For the user to levitate any of their body parts, their feet must remain on the ground, as they are the only body parts incapable of levitation. This can be somewhat rectified if someone else carries their feet, allowing them to stand on that person. However, Buggy has been seen running in midair while separated at the waist, which raises questions about continuity.

Additionally, the user is affected by the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses, including the inability to swim.

Usage

Originally, after finding the fruit, Buggy intended to sell it to fund his search for an undersea treasure but ended up eating it by accident when Shanks startled him. Since then, Buggy has exploited the fruit's powers to gather treasure on land.

Buggy has used his fruit's powers for fighting, protecting himself, and punishing those who fail or annoy him. His versatile abilities mean he is often used by others for convenience, such as a decoy against swordsmen or as a method of transportation due to his floating abilities. Buggy's ability to split his body automatically triggers when cut, making him fearless towards swordsmen, contrary to his usual cowardice against more powerful enemies.

Techniques

The named techniques that involve the Devil Fruit powers include:

  • Bara Bara Ho: Buggy shoots his hands at the enemy.
  • Chi o Hau Bara Bara Ho: A variation where Buggy shoots his hand across the ground to grab an opponent's legs.
  • Bara Bara Ho Kirihanashi: Buggy adds part of his forearm to his fist, allowing him to detach it and continue the attack.
  • Bara Bara Senbei: Buggy throws his lower half cartwheeling towards an opponent.
  • Bara Bara Kinkyu Dasshutsu: Buggy separates his head from his body to avoid an incoming attack.
  • Bara Bara Festival: Buggy separates all his body parts and spins them around to attack enemies.
  • Bara Bara Car: Buggy reconfigures himself into a small car for quick getaways.
  • Kuchu Kirimomi Dai Circus: Buggy detaches his hands to grab an opponent and sends them crashing to the ground.

History

At least 27 years ago, when Buggy was a pirate apprentice of the Roger Pirates, the crew came across the Bara Bara no Mi. Buggy initially showed no interest in eating it but accidentally swallowed it when startled by Shanks. This incident led to Buggy's decision to use his new powers to gather treasure on land instead of seeking it under the sea.

Trivia

A fan once asked Oda if Buggy's penis can split off and fly, which Oda confirmed. Oda also claimed that if he could eat any Devil Fruit, it would be the Bara Bara no Mi, although he later suggested the Suke Suke no Mi. Buggy is immune to Donquixote Doflamingo's Torikago, as he can slip past the slicing threads unharmed. The Bara Bara no Mi can be considered the opposite of the Gomu Gomu no Mi, as Luffy is immune to blunt attacks while Buggy is immune to slicing attacks.
