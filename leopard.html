Statistics

Japanese Name: ネコネコの実 モデル“豹レオパルド”

Romanized Name: Neko Neko no Mi, Moderu: Reoparudo

Official English Name: Cat-Cat Fruit Leopard Model (VIZ); Cat-Cat Fruit, Model: Leopard (VIZ, Funimation)

Meaning: Cat

Fruit Debut: One Piece Magazine Vol.16; Episode 1101

Usage Debut: Chapter 348; Episode 245

Type: Zoan

Current User: Rob Lucci

Etymology

"Neko" is Japanese for "cat." While the kanji is written as 豹 (hyō, Japanese for "leopard"), the pronunciation remains as reoparudo, the Japanese way to pronounce the species' name. It is called the Cat-Cat Fruit Leopard Model in the VIZ Manga until its awakening in Chapter 1069. It is called the Cat-Cat Fruit, Model: Leopard in the VIZ Manga as of Chapter 1069 and both Unlimited Adventure and the English adaptations of the anime series, which are both dubbed by Funimation.

Appearance

The Neko Neko no Mi, Model: Leopard resembles the head of a leopard, including spots and ears. The insides of the ears have swirls. The fruit also has a green T-shaped stem that curls up at one end.

Transformations

The fruit allows for multiple transformations, including:

  • Beast Form
  • Human-Beast Form
  • Awakened Human-Beast Form

    • Strengths and Weaknesses

    The fruit's major strength, as demonstrated by Lucci, is that its user gains a dramatic increase in physical strength in their hybrid and full leopard forms, equivalent to that of an actual leopard and more. In his hybrid form, Lucci was capable of hurling Luffy and Zoro through a building and across the city of Water 7. Their half-leopard form is extremely large, as the user's original height and mass are significantly increased. Fighting styles and abilities, like Rokushiki, are greatly enhanced by the power of the fruit.

    The fruit grants the user the abilities of a leopard, such as enhanced strength, durability, speed, agility, powerful teeth and jaws, retractable claws, stealth, enhanced senses, and enhanced climbing and jumping capabilities.

    As stated by Tony Tony Chopper, Zoan that turn into carnivorous animals are more ferocious than others by nature. The fruit does not seem to have any specific weaknesses outside the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses.

    Awakening

    Upon awakening, the user's Human-Beast form undergoes a physical transformation where their size increases, their body becomes more slender and muscular, fangs develop, a dark flame forms a "mane" down the user's back and arms, and a stream of floating fire wraps around the user's shoulders. Unique among Zoan Fruits, this "awakening" should have taken control of the user's personality, but Rob Lucci maintained his.

    Usage

    The powers of the fruit have been used mostly by Rob Lucci for combat purposes and to further fuel his lust for battle. Lucci notably uses the fruit's powers to enhance his Rokushiki techniques like Shigan and Rankyaku with the addition of claws on his fingers and the added strength in his legs respectively. Lucci has also used the fruit's power in a more bestial manner, attacking Luffy like a real leopard with claws and teeth.

    While most Zoan Devil Fruits normally allow three forms of transformations, Lucci is able to access a fourth form using Seimei Kikan. Through Seimei Kikan: Kami-e Bushin, Lucci's muscles shrink and compress, granting him a smaller and slightly more human half-leopard form, which is faster but has lower brute strength.

    Techniques

    Kamisori (剃刀, Kamisori, literally meaning "Razor"): A combination of Geppo and Soru, allowing extremely fast movements in three dimensions. In the VIZ Manga, this is called Razor.

    Rankyaku "Hyobi"(嵐脚「豹尾」, Rankyaku "Hyōbi", literally meaning "Storm Leg: 'Leopard Tail'"): Rob Lucci creates a large, powerful Rankyaku in the shape of a swirl, launched at the enemy like a razor blade, capable of stunning and sending opponents flying.

    Tekkai "Utsugi" (鉄塊「空木」, Tekkai "Utsugi", literally meaning "Iron Mass: 'Deutzia'"): This form of Tekkai is used as a counter, transferring the shock of an attack back to the attacker.

    Tobu Shigan "Bachi" (飛ぶ指銃「撥」, Tobu Shigan "Bachi", literally meaning "Flying Finger Gun: 'Plectrum'"): A piercing attack launched by flicking, capable of piercing Luffy's rubber body.

    Rankyaku "Gaicho" (嵐脚「凱鳥」, Rankyaku "Gaichō", literally meaning "Storm Leg: 'Victorious Bird'"): A large Rankyaku created using claws in half-leopard form, with a wider range and greater attack power.

    Seimei Kikan: Kami-e Bushin (生命帰還 紙絵武身, Seimei Kikan: Kami-e Bushin, literally meaning "Life/Existence Return: Paper Drawing Martial Body"): A transformation that makes Lucci smaller and lighter, enhancing agility and maneuverability.

    Shigan "Madara" (指銃「斑」, Shigan "Madara", literally meaning "Finger Gun: 'Speckles'"): A barrage of attacks performed with both hands using clawed fingers.

    Rokushiki Ogi: Rokuogan (六式奥義 六王銃, Rokushiki Ōgi: Rokuōgan, literally meaning "Six Styles Secret Skill: Six King Gun"): An ultimate technique that can damage an opponent internally, powerful enough to cause severe injury.

    Sai Dai Rin: Rokuogan (最大輪 六王銃, Sai Dai Rin: Rokuōgan, literally meaning "Maximum Ring: Six King Gun"): Used in hybrid form, this technique restrains the target before delivering a powerful attack.

    Awakened Techniques

    Shugan (手銃シュガン, Shugan, literally meaning "Hand Gun"): In Awakened Form, Lucci stabs the opponent with his whole hand.

    Shugan: Madara (手銃シュガン 斑マダラ, Shugan: Madara, literally meaning "Hand Gun: Speckles"): A rapid barrage of attacks performed with both hands in Awakened Form.

    Anime-Only Techniques

    Tobu Shigan "Mitsubachi" (飛ぶ指銃「三撥」, Tobu Shigan "Mitsubachi", literally meaning "Flying Finger Gun: 'Three Plectrums'"): Fires the Tobu Shigan: Bachi three times consecutively.

    Tobu Shigan "Hibachi" (飛ぶ指銃「火撥」, Tobu Shigan "Hibachi", literally meaning "Flying Finger Gun: 'Fire Plectrum'"): Similar to Tobu Shigan: Bachi, but the projectile is a fireball that burns the victim upon impact.

    History

    Some time prior to the Egghead Incident, Rob Lucci awakened the fruit's powers.
