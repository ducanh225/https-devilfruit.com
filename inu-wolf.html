Home > Zoan > Inu Inu no Mi, Model: Wolf

Statistics

Japanese Name: イヌイヌの実 モデル「狼ウルフ」

Romanized Name: Inu Inu no Mi, Moderu: Urufu

Official English Name: Mutt-Mutt Fruit Wolf Model (Viz); Dog-Dog Fruit Model Wolf (Funimation)

Meaning: Dog

Usage Debut: Chapter 401; Episode 286

Type: Zoan

Current User: Jabra

Etymology

"Inu" is Japanese for "dog," and wolves are in the same family as dogs. While the kanji is written as 狼 (ōkami, Japanese for "wolf"), the pronunciation remains as urufu, the Japanese way to pronounce the species' name. In the VIZ manga, it is called the Mutt-Mutt Fruit Wolf Model. In the FUNimation adaptations, it is called the Dog-Dog Fruit, Model: Wolf.

Strengths and Weaknesses

The fruit's major strength, as demonstrated by Jabra, is that its user gains more physical strength in their hybrid and full wolf forms, equivalent to that of a real wolf and more. Any physical fighting style that the user has, like the Rokushiki, is generally made more lethal with the additional strength bestowed upon them. The user is also granted additional weapons of claws and fangs, which can be used effectively in attacks, greatly increasing the potency of their normal fighting style techniques, as demonstrated with Jabra's far more lethal Shigan while in half-wolf form. The fruit does not seem to have any specific weaknesses outside the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses.

Usage

The powers of the fruit have been used mostly by Jabra for combat purposes in conjunction with Rokushiki and Tekkai Kenpo to make him a more lethal fighter. When used together with his underhanded tactics to make opponents drop their guard, they give Jabra an advantage that most predators in the wild have.

While not battling, Jabra has also used the Devil Fruit powers to help him in non-assassin based circumstances. To help his strongest fellow CP9 agent, Rob Lucci, after the incident in Enies Lobby, Jabra used his Devil Fruit powers to help raise Lucci's medical funds. While in his full wolf form, Jabra performed a street act where he jumped through hoops of fire to thrill passersby and earn money for Lucci's cause.

Techniques

Jusshigan (十指銃, Jusshigan, literally meaning "Ten Finger Guns"): In this attack, Jabra puts both of his hands together and performs Shigan with all ten of his fingers at once, greatly damaging the opponent. In the VIZ manga and Funimation adaptations, this is called Ten Finger Pistol.

Gekko Jusshigan (月光十指銃, Gekkō Jusshigan, literally meaning "Moonlight Ten Finger Guns"): A combination of Jusshigan and Geppo. After using Geppo to get into the air and avoid an attack, Jabra launches himself towards his airborne target in a vertical drop. The added momentum makes the following Jusshigan more effective and harder to avoid. In the VIZ manga and Funimation adaptations, this is called Moonlight Ten Finger Pistol.

Tekkai Kenpo (鉄塊 拳法, Tekkai Kenpō, literally meaning "Iron Mass Fist Law"): Using Tekkai Kenpo, Jabra can move while using Tekkai. Tekkai Kenpo is an entire fighting style with various techniques of its own. In the VIZ manga, this is called Iron Body Kung Fu, and in the FUNimation adaptations, it is called Iron Body Kenpo.

Tekkai Kenpo "Okami Hajiki" (鉄塊拳法「狼弾」, Tekkai Kenpō "Ōkami Hajiki", literally meaning "Iron Mass Fist Law: 'Wolf Bullet'"): After hardening his hands with Tekkai, Jabra punches his opponent. In the VIZ manga, this is called Iron Body Kung Fu Wolf Bullet. In the FUNimation adaptations, this is called Iron Body Kenpo Wolf Repel.

Tekkai Kenpo "Roga no Kamae" (鉄塊拳法「狼牙の構え」, Tekkai Kenpō "Rōga no Kamae", literally meaning "Iron Mass Fist Law: 'Stance of Wolf Fang'"): Rōga is one of Jabra's forms of Tekkai Kenpō, where he attacks his opponent using his claws. The way Rōga is called is a pun in Japanese, often ending sentences with darō, meaning "probably." Jabra replaces darō with da rōga. In the VIZ manga, this is called Iron Body Kung Fu Wolf Fang Stance.

Tekkai Kenpo "Roba no Kamae" (鉄塊拳法「狼芭の構え」, Tekkai Kenpō "Rōba no Kamae", literally meaning "Iron Mass Fist Law: 'Stance of Wolf Banana'"): A form of Tekkai Kenpō, where Jabra combines Tekkai with Soru to move at extreme speeds. In the VIZ manga, this is called Iron Body Kung Fu Wolf Plantain Stance.

Rokaru Area Network (狼狩エリア・ネットワーク, Rōkaru Eria Nettowāku, literally meaning "Wolf Hunting Area Network"): While moving at high speed, Jabra slashes at his opponent with both hands, appearing to attack with multiple hands. "Rokaru" is a pun on "local area network." In the VIZ manga and FUNimation adaptations, this is called Wolf Hunt Highspeed Scratch.

Tekkai Kenpo "Don Poro" (鉄塊拳法「重歩狼」, Tekkai Kenpō "Don Pōrō", literally meaning "Iron Mass Fist Law: 'Heavy Step Wolf'"): A punch to the opponent's stomach while using Tekkai. In the VIZ manga and Funimation subs, this is called Iron Body Kung Fu Don Po Ro.

Tekkai Kenpo "Matenro" (鉄塊拳法「摩天狼」, Tekkai Kenpō "Matenrō", literally meaning "Iron Mass Fist Law: 'Demon Sirius/Heaven Wolf'"): Jabra flips onto his hands and kicks his opponent into the air. In the VIZ manga, this is called Iron Body Kung Fu Devil Wolf.

Rankyaku "Koro" (嵐脚「孤狼」, Rankyaku "Korō", literally meaning "Storm Leg: 'Lone Wolf'"): In this attack, the Rankyaku-cut skips across the ground, bouncing in curves towards the opponent. In the VIZ manga and Funimation adaptations, this is called Tempest Kick Lone Wolf.

Rankyaku "Lupus Fall" (嵐脚「群狼連星ルプスフォール」, Rankyaku "Rupusu Fōru", literally meaning "Storm Leg: 'Swarm Wolf Binary Star'"): In this powerful attack, Jabra shoots four Rankyaku projectiles shaped like wolves at the opponent. These projectiles seem to explode upon contact rather than cutting, similar to compressed air bullets. In the VIZ manga and FUNimation adaptations, this is called Tempest Kick Lupus Fall.

Trivia

Though not exactly verified, Jabra claims that when he turns into his hybrid form and gains more physical strength, his doriki level also increases due to the Devil Fruit's powers. This is first seen during a short altercation between Jabra and Lucci when they insult each other's animal themes after returning to Enies Lobby.
