Statistics

Japanese Name: バクバクの実

Official English Name: Munch-Munch Fruit

Baku Baku Fruit: *

Meaning: Sound of Chomping

Fruit Debut: One Piece Magazine Vol.9

Usage Debut: Chapter 131; Episode 79

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Wapol

Description

The Baku Baku no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to eat otherwise inedible objects, then later manifest them from their body. It was eaten by Wapol, king of the Evil Black Drum Kingdom.

Etymology

"Bakubaku" (バクバク/ばくばく) is the Japanese onomatopoeia for loud and extreme eating, particularly chewing motions. In the VIZ Manga release, and the 4Kids and Funimation dubs, it is called the Munch-Munch Fruit, reflecting the sound effects that derive from this fruit's actions.

Appearance

The Baku Baku no Mi is a purple, plum-shaped fruit that is split by a zigzag pattern in the center, giving it the appearance of a set of jaws. It has a green, curly stem at the top and the typical swirl patterns on its peel.

Strengths and Weaknesses

The fruit's major strength, as demonstrated by Wapol, is that the user gains the ability to stretch open their mouths to large sizes and eat things in massive proportions. It also strengthens their teeth and digestive system, allowing them to consume things that would otherwise be inedible or toxic to regular people, ranging from inanimate objects such as weapons to living creatures such as humans. The user can then transform any part of their bodies into whatever they had eaten, enabling them to produce weapons for offensive capabilities, or they can fuse objects or creatures together to create new things.

While the devoured matter allows the user to give a great variety of applications in battle and out, it gives the user perpetual hunger that cannot be satiated by eating. "Chewy" foods, such as rubber, are slightly harder for the user to swallow. Additionally, the user can only manifest objects eaten in the past 24 hours; any objects eaten before that cannot be recovered. Other than that, the user is also affected by the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses and presumably cannot eat Seastone, which would nullify the user's powers.

Usage

The fruit's bestowed powers, as used by Wapol, have been primarily to help him engorge himself on various things, including knives and even the houses of his former subjects. As he often eats other people's property, he frequently causes great annoyance to those around him. Wapol can also use the fruit's powers for combat purposes by either swallowing his opponent whole or by using the fruit's transformation powers to aid him. He even managed to survive an avalanche by swallowing snow masses and keeping his subordinates safe in his mouth.

After being defeated by Luffy and becoming a vagrant, Wapol was able to come up with more benevolent applications of his fruit's powers, serving more productive purposes. Using his Baku Baku Factory technique, Wapol was able to produce toys to sell for a living, creating a new industry for him to rule over.

Techniques

Yukigesho (雪化粧, Yukigeshō): Wapol hides himself in the snowy landscape of Drum Island and ambushes trespassers by jumping out with his jaws wide open, ready to swallow them. He can also hide Chess and Kuromarimo as backup if this attack fails.

Baku Baku Shock (バクバク食ショック, Baku Baku Shokku): After eating something, Wapol can mutate the object or material into his body, such as turning his arm into a cannon. This was first seen when Wapol turned himself into a house with cannons for arms.

Wapol House (ワポルハウス, Waporu Hausu): After consuming various food items, Wapol absorbs them into his body and transforms into a house complete with doors, chimney, and windows. His arms are also transformed into cannons, and the chimney can act as a cannon.

Bero Cannon (ベロ大砲キャノン, Bero Kyanon): Wapol transforms his tongue into a cannon and fires a cannonball at his opponent. This was first seen when he fired at Luffy while being held by the jaw.

Non-Canon Techniques

Arm Cannon (アーム大砲キャノン, Āmu Kyanon): Wapol transforms his arm or both arms into cannons and fires cannonballs at his opponent. This technique is only named in One Piece: Unlimited Cruise and Pirate Warriors.

Shiro Wapol (城ワポル, Shiro Waporu): After eating a castle and ten cannons, Wapol transforms into a castle with giant cannons for arms. This ability was used in One Piece Party.

Wapol 10-ren Cannon (ワポル10連キャノン, Waporu 10-ren Kyanon): Using all ten cannons at once, Wapol bombards his opponents.

Bakudan Wapol (爆弾ワポル, Bakudan Waporu): After eating a giant bomb, Wapol transforms into it. While he can cause the bomb to explode, he is not immune to it and damages himself in the process.

History

Wapol's journey with the Baku Baku no Mi began when he consumed the fruit, granting him its unique powers. He used these abilities to assert his dominance as king and later to survive after his defeat by Luffy.

Trivia

A fan once asked Oda what would happen if Wapol ate Luffy and combined them together like Chessmarimo. Oda humorously illustrated this combination, naming it "Gargon." This amalgamation possessed features from all the Straw Hat members.

Despite the natural law of Devil Fruits dictating that a person can only have one Devil Fruit power at a time, Wapol's ability to fuse multiple beings together raises questions about this rule. This aspect was explored in the ninth One Piece movie.

The Baku Baku no Mi's ability to consume inedible materials is reminiscent of the eating disorder pica, where individuals compulsively eat non-food items.
