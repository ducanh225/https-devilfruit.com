Fruit Debut: Chapter 689; Episode 615

Usage Debut: Chapter 689; Episode 615

Type: Artificial Zoan

Current User: Gifters; Various people

Appearance

SMILEs resemble the artificial Devil Fruit that Vegapunk created; they are colorful fruits resembling apples that have spots of a different color on them, and those spots have another spot inside each of them.

Production

The key ingredient required to concoct the artificial fruits is SAD, which is a chemical substance created by mixing the extracted Lineage Factors of numerous animals together. Only Caesar Clown possesses knowledge of how to create SAD, and as such, the production of these fake Devil Fruits requires Caesar's cooperation. After Caesar had produced the SAD in his lab on Punk Hazard, it was then transferred to the SMILE Factory in Dressrosa and processed into SMILEs.

The manufacturing process of the SMILE Factory involves having luminescent sunflowers on the ceiling that provide light to the ordinary fruit trees growing in the factory's indoor orchard. Additionally, the SAD is poured into an indoor river that feeds into irrigation canals, channeling the river under the trees to feed them the chemical, modifying the fruits that grow from them.

Due to the Tontatta Tribe's dwarves specializing in plants, they were forced into labor in the factory producing SMILEs. It appears that the Tontatta could only produce one viable fruit out of every ten SMILEs, and even they themselves noted the unorthodox nature of the fruit had complicated the success rate. With the destruction of both the SMILE factory and SAD supply, the production of SMILEs has stopped.

Attributes

A SMILE fruit is intended to grant the consumer the power of animal transformation, much like a Zoan-type Devil Fruit, by a process which involves the transmission of the Lineage Factor of a real animal. Like normal Devil Fruits, SMILEs render those who consume them unable to swim. It has been noted that, unlike real Devil Fruits, SMILE fruits actually have a good flavor.

SMILEs grant enhanced physical abilities, much like real Zoan fruits. However, many, if not all, of these artificial Devil Fruits appear to be greatly inferior in strength compared to actual Zoan fruits.

SMILE Success Rate

As noted by Law, SMILEs, being artificial, do have side effects. Due to the faulty chemicals used to make them, only one out of every ten SMILE fruits will successfully grant Zoan-like abilities at the cost of being able to swim. The other 90% of people who consume one will not receive a power while suffering the usual consequences. Notably, SMILE users are susceptible to the power of the Kibi Kibi no Mi, which allows the user to tame animals by feeding them the treats created via this power.

Differences from Natural Zoans

While normal Zoan users can fully transform into an animal, SMILE users have demonstrated the ability to turn certain parts of their body into parts of the animal's body. Some transformations are so extreme that it looks more like the human is growing out of the animal rather than the other way around. SMILE users can achieve advanced control over their powers, but it is uncertain what that looks like.

    History

    The SMILE fruits were invented by Caesar Clown, who developed a method to create artificial Zoan-type Devil Fruits with a special chemical known as SAD. After the SMILEs were created at the factory in Dressrosa, they were monopolized by Kaidou, who fed them to his crew members to turn them into Gifters. The members of the Beasts Pirates who ate defective SMILEs became known as the Pleasures, forced to express constant joy regardless of their actual feelings.

    Trivia

    The side effects of eating a defective SMILE fruit may be a reference to the concept of Joker Venom, a weapon used by the DC Comics supervillain, the Joker. The SMILEs were named as such due to the cruel irony of their effects, as 90% of the eaters ended up smiling and laughing for the rest of their lives. The Gifters' ability to turn selective parts of their body into animals is similar to an advanced level of control of one's Zoan Devil Fruit powers.
