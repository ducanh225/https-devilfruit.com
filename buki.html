Statistics

Japanese Name: ブキブキの実

Romanized Name: Buki Buki no Mi

Official English Name: Arms-Arms Fruit

Meaning: Weapon

Usage Debut: Chapter 682; Episode 608

Type: Paramecia

Current User: Baby 5

Description

The Buki Buki no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that gives the user the ability to change their body parts into any weapon, allowing them to become a Full-Body Weapon Human (全身武器人間, Zenshin Buki Ningen). It was eaten by Baby 5.

Etymology

"Buki" (武器) means "weapon" in Japanese. In the Viz Manga and FUNimation adaptations, it is called the Arms-Arms Fruit, with "arms" coming from "armaments."

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

The Buki Buki no Mi allows the user to change their body into any kind of weapon. The user can transform small parts of their body, like fingers and hands, or larger sections, such as their entire torso or even their whole body, into a weapon. This ability makes it unnecessary for the user to carry or utilize external weapons.

While transformed into a weapon, the user does not take any damage from the weapon's impact, such as detonating while in the state of an explosive. If the user's body is broken apart, the pieces can easily reform into the user's full body, undamaged, akin to a Logia. However, this regeneration does not apply to the user's human form, even if they are partially transformed.

Weaknesses

Although transforming into self-detonating weapons does not harm the user, they remain vulnerable to the generated weapons if aimed at themselves, voluntarily or otherwise. Other than that, this fruit has no apparent weaknesses aside from the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses.

Usage

As an assassin, Baby 5 uses this fruit's power for assassination and combat at both long and close range. She can transform small parts of her body, like her finger into a gun, or turn half of her body into a sword or even transmute her entire body into a rocket or explosive. She usually transforms her arms and, on occasion, her legs into various types of blades or firearms to complement her hand-to-hand combat. Partnered with Buffalo, Baby 5's weapons gain increased momentum and damage range.

Despite having this power, Baby 5 still carries external weapons due to her personality flaw, having bought extra weapons from a salesman who sweet-talked her into it.

Techniques

The named techniques that involve the Devil Fruit powers include:

Buki-morphose (武器変貌, Bukimorufōze): Baby 5 transforms part of her body into the weapon she wants to be. In the Viz Manga and Funimation adaptations, this is called Arms-morphosis.

Espada Girl (剣女エスパーダ・ガール, Esupāda Gāru): Baby 5 turns into a large sword, with her legs serving as the handle.

Missile Girl (ミサイル女ガール, Misairu Gāru): Baby 5 turns into a large missile, launched by Buffalo using his "Guru Guru Toshaho."

Revolver Girl (拳銃女レボルベルガール, Reboruberu Gāru): Baby 5 turns into a giant gun barrel and fires rapidly.

Sickle Girl (鎌女シックル・ガール, Shikkuru Gāru): Baby 5 transforms into a kusarigama-like weapon, allowing her to be spun around and used by Buffalo.

History

At least 13 years ago, Baby 5 ate the Buki Buki no Mi, using its ability against Wellington when she was 11.

Trivia

The Buki Buki no Mi has similarities with other Devil Fruits due to its weapon-producing capabilities. It is similar to the Baku Baku no Mi, as both allow the user to turn their bodies into weapons; the main difference is that the Baku Baku no Mi is limited to the weapons the user devours. It also shares similarities with the Supa Supa no Mi, Ute Ute no Mi, and Bomu Bomu no Mi, as it can produce blades, guns, and explosives, respectively.

According to Oda, Franky would have this Devil Fruit if he was allowed one.
